FASHION BLOG

Slip dresses are a versatile and effortless option for spring and summer. Opt for a lightweight fabric like satin or silk in a neutral shade like beige or ivory. Layer it over a fitted turtleneck or t-shirt for a cool , modern look, or wear it alone with strappy sandals for a minimalist yet chic ensemble.
